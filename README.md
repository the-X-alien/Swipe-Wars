## PLAY!
How to win
Be the richest player when the timer ends.
Use your Microbit card to earn, spend, steal, and sabotage.


## Who goes first?
Each player rolls the die.
The highest roller starts.
On your turn
Roll the die.
Move your token clockwise that number of spaces.
Land on a tile and perform the action using your Microbit card.
Swipe at the terminal to confirm transactions.
